# SITE_STRUCTURE.md

## Overview

The Nuclii website is a clean, animated pre-launch marketing site.

Main navigation:

1. Home
2. About
3. Early Access
4. For Hosts
5. Build With Us
6. Get in Touch

Footer-only pages:

1. Investors
2. Privacy Policy
3. Terms of Use
4. Cookie Policy
5. Community Guidelines

Do not overload the main navigation. The main user journey should always lead toward Early Access.

---

# 1. Home Page

Route: `/`

## Purpose

The Home page should explain Nuclii quickly, make the product feel premium, and push people toward Early Access.

## Primary CTA

- Join Early Access

## Secondary CTA

- Explore Nuclii
- Become an Early Host

## Sections

### 1. Hero

Headline:

> Every event starts here.

Subtext:

> Nuclii brings together attendees, hosts, communities, services, and venues into one platform built for real-life experiences.

Visual direction:

- dark background
- blue animated map-inspired background
- floating event/listing cards
- glowing location pins
- phone mockup showing map discovery
- clear Join Early Access CTA

Small badges:

- UK Launch
- Privacy-first
- Built for real life

### 2. What is Nuclii?

Explain:

> Nuclii helps people discover, host, book, and manage real-world experiences without relying on followers, group chats, or social pressure.

Use four cards:

- Discover experiences
- Host with structure
- Book or RSVP
- Access safely

### 3. Why Nuclii exists

Problem statements:

- experiences are scattered across DMs, flyers, WhatsApp, Instagram stories, and group chats
- small hosts struggle to reach the right people
- attendees miss things because they are not connected enough
- private gatherings and small services need safer structure

### 4. Product preview

Show app-style mockups:

- map discovery
- listing detail
- ticket wallet
- QR check-in
- host dashboard

### 5. Use cases preview

Use cards for:

- society events
- workshops
- private gatherings
- food pickups
- barber/braider/lash/nail slots
- pop-ups
- talent showcases
- venue collaborations

### 6. Safety preview

Show trust points:

- anonymous-by-default public identity
- no public attendee lists
- eligibility-aware discovery
- privacy-aware location reveal
- QR access without exposing unnecessary identity

### 7. Final CTA

Headline:

> Be part of the first Nuclii community.

Button:

- Join Early Access

# 3. Early Access Page

Route: `/early-access`

## Purpose

This is the most important conversion page.

It is the waitlist and marketplace seeding page. It should help populate both sides of the app before launch:

- demand side: attendees/users
- supply side: hosts, societies, service providers, talent, venues

## Primary CTA

- Start Early Access Form

## Sections

### 1. Hero

Headline:

> Join the first wave of Nuclii.

Subtext:

> Get early access as an attendee, host, society, service provider, talent, venue, or partner.

### 2. Choose your path

Use large interactive cards:

1. I want to attend
2. I want to host
3. I run a society/community
4. I provide a service
5. I represent a venue
6. I want to partner/invest

Each card should have a CTA:

- Join as an Attendee
- Apply as an Early Host
- Register a Society
- Register as a Service Provider
- Register a Venue
- Contact the Nuclii Team

### 3. Multi-step form

Fields:

- full name
- email
- city
- university or organisation, optional
- role
- intended use
- 16+ confirmation
- consent to receive Nuclii updates

Role options:

- attendee
- host
- society/community
- service provider
- talent/creative
- venue/business
- partner
- investor
- team/contributor
- other

### 4. Confirmation state

After submission:

> You’re on the list.
> We’ll contact selected early users, hosts, and partners as Nuclii prepares for beta.

Do not show fake waitlist numbers.
