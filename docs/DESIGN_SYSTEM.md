# DESIGN_SYSTEM.md

## Visual Direction

Nuclii should look clean, premium, animated, and trustworthy.

The website should feel like:

- a modern startup product
- a map-first real-world experience platform
- a serious infrastructure layer
- a clean pre-launch website
- energetic but not noisy

The visual identity should be blue / black / white.

Avoid making the site too purple, green, neon-heavy, childish, or club/nightlife-focused.

## Colour Usage

Use black and charcoal for most backgrounds.

Use electric blue for:

- primary CTAs
- active states
- animated map pins
- glows
- selected cards
- form focus rings
- important tags

Use white for:

- primary text
- light sections
- legal pages/content panels

Use grey for:

- secondary text
- borders
- muted descriptions

Purple may be used only as a subtle background glow if needed.

Avoid green except for success states.

---

## Typography

Preferred typography:

- Headings: Clash Display or a similar display font
- Body: Satoshi or Inter

If custom fonts are not available, use:

- Headings: Inter Tight, Space Grotesk, or Geist Sans
- Body: Inter or Geist Sans

Typography style:

- large confident headlines
- readable body text
- short paragraphs
- clear CTA labels
- generous line height

Avoid long dense text blocks on marketing pages.

---

## Layout

Use:

- strong responsive grid systems
- generous whitespace
- max-width containers
- large rounded cards
- bento grids
- alternating dark/light sections where useful
- sticky navigation
- clear section hierarchy

Recommended container widths:

- main container: `max-w-7xl`
- text-heavy pages: `max-w-4xl`
- legal pages: `max-w-3xl` or `max-w-4xl`

Recommended section spacing:

- desktop: `py-24` to `py-32`
- tablet: `py-20`
- mobile: `py-14` to `py-16`

---

## Cards

Cards should feel premium and soft.

Card style:

- rounded corners: `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl`
- background: dark surface or translucent black
- border: subtle grey border
- shadow: soft blue/black shadow
- hover: slight lift, border blue, background glow

Use glassmorphism sparingly:

- navbar
- floating UI cards
- hero overlays
- product preview panels

Avoid making every component glassy.

---

## Buttons

Primary button:

- electric blue background
- white text
- rounded-full or rounded-xl
- strong hover glow
- subtle scale or magnetic hover

Secondary button:

- transparent or dark background
- subtle border
- white text
- blue hover border

CTA text examples:

- Join Early Access
- Become an Early Host
- Build With Us
- Get in Touch
- Request Investor Information

Button behaviour:

- clear focus ring
- accessible contrast
- no tiny text
- consistent sizing

---

## Navigation

Navbar:

- sticky top
- blur background
- Nuclii logo left
- nav links center or right
- primary CTA right
- mobile menu

Main nav links:

- Home
- About
- Early Access
- For Hosts
- Build With Us
- Get in Touch

Footer links:

- Investors
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Use
- Cookie Policy
- Community Guidelines

Do not clutter navbar with legal/investor links.

---

## Animation Style

Nuclii should be animation-esque, but still clean.

Use:

- smooth scroll reveals
- floating cards
- subtle map pin pulses
- glowing gradient orbs
- animated dotted map background
- card hover lifts
- form step transitions
- device mockup parallax
- accordion transitions
- tab transitions

Avoid:

- aggressive bouncing
- constant spinning
- childish cartoon motion
- too many particle effects
- heavy 3D effects that slow the site

Motion should feel like a premium product site.

---

## Hero Visual System

The hero should include:

- dark background
- blue map-inspired abstract grid
- floating event/listing cards
- glowing pins
- phone/app mockup
- strong CTA

The waitlist CTA must be impossible to miss.

Do not overcrowd the hero with too many badges or statistics.

---

## Product Mockups

Use mockups to suggest the product before launch.

Suggested mock screens:

- map discovery
- listing detail
- ticket wallet
- QR access
- host dashboard
- early access form

Mockups should be realistic but not overly detailed.

Do not show features that contradict the product strategy.

---

## Forms

Forms should be clean, premium, and multi-step where useful.

Form style:

- clear labels
- large input fields
- rounded corners
- strong focus states
- helpful placeholder text
- short forms
- progress indicator for multi-step forms

Important forms:

- Early Access form
- Build With Us application form
- Get in Touch form
- Investor enquiry form

Early Access form must include:

- 16+ confirmation
- consent to receive updates

---

## Icons

Use clean line icons.

Recommended icon style:

- Lucide icons
- simple map pins
- ticket icons
- QR icons
- shield/privacy icons
- calendar icons
- user/group icons
- building/venue icons

Do not use cartoonish icons.

---

## Responsive Behaviour

Mobile is important.

Mobile requirements:

- navbar collapses cleanly
- CTA visible near top
- hero text remains readable
- phone mockups do not overcrowd
- cards stack properly
- forms are easy to complete
- footer is simple and readable

Avoid horizontal overflow.

---

## Accessibility

Minimum requirements:

- semantic HTML
- labelled form fields
- accessible button labels
- keyboard navigable menus
- focus states
- sufficient colour contrast
- alt text for meaningful images
- no motion that prevents usability

Respect reduced motion preferences where possible.
